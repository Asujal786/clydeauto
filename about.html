About US

Welcome to Clyde Auto Mechanic, your trusted partner in vehicle maintenance and repair. We are a team of experienced and certified auto mechanics dedicated to providing top-notch service to our customers.Our team has decades of combined experience in the auto industry, and we stay up-to-date with the latest technologies and trends in the field. We use state-of-the-art equipment and high-quality parts to ensure that your vehicle receives the best possible care.

Established in 2010, Clyde Auto Mechanic has been serving the Sydney community for over a decade. We are conveniently located at 306 Elizabeth St, Sydney, making us easily accessible to residents and businesses in the area. At Clyde Auto Mechanic, we understand how important your vehicle is to you. It's not just a means of transportation, but also a part of your daily routine and lifestyle. That's why we offer a wide range of services to keep your vehicle running smoothly and safely, from routine maintenance to complex repairs. We believe in transparency and communication, and we strive to keep you informed every step of the way. We will explain the issues with your vehicle and provide you with detailed recommendations and estimates, so you can make informed decisions about your vehicle's maintenance and repairs.We take pride in our work and stand behind our services with a satisfaction guarantee. Whether you need a simple oil change, brake repair, or engine diagnostics, we are here to help.

Thank you for choosing Clyde Auto Mechanic. We look forward to serving you and your vehicle!
